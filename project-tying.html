Tying a knot in time's tail

Performance / 2025 / 5h

Statement

I do not experience spacetime as a single continuous line — but as something that accumulates, layer upon layer. With every passing second, another fold of time and space is added to the ones before it.Each minute, time lets fall a small tail.

And so I spent one minute stretching a strip of candy taffy into a long, thin strand — and at the end of that minute, I tied it to the strand from the minute before. Then the next, and the next, weaving them together into a necklace.

Across different pockets of spacetime, I knotted time's tails to one another, attempting to stitch together the scattered fragments of what has been.
