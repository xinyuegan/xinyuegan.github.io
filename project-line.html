A Line

Performance / 2025

Statement

I believe that people are connected to one another by lines.

Between my mother's body and mine — perhaps navel to navel, cord to cord — there runs a single line. One end lives in my room on the seventh floor. The other trails down to the walkway of the residential compound below.

When I am not looking, my mother pulls that line — hard. I will be mid-stride, walking, when I feel a sudden and violent tug dragging me backwards, step by reluctant step. No matter how far I walk, I cannot leave this compound. Just as she, no matter how she wanders, cannot leave my room.

And yet, beneath the open blue of the sky, there are countless threads — dense and trembling, pulling in every direction at once. The world is a net, and it breathes.
