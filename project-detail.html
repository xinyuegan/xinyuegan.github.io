Words don't mean what you think they mean

Film Installation/ 2024 / Ink, rice papper, black dust tape

Statement

This installation is created for the experimental video of the same title. Within this work, I further expand the meaning of language, transforming it into physical entity, into traces, into an emergent chaos suspended between recognizable and unrecognizable.
