Untitled

Performance Art / 2025 / 10h

Statement

I spent six hours tearing stacked cardboard boxes from my childhood into fragments, then adhered them into a solid cube. Between destruction and reassembly, the cardboard underwent a shift from structure to material. Emptiness turned into solidity, functionality vanished, and pure materiality emerged.
