The Ideal Artist’s Studio

Sculpture / 2023 / Mixed media

Statement

I created a small-scale miniature model for the exhibition The Ideal Artist's Studio, which explores and challenges public perceptions of what constitutes an ideal artist's workspace. In collaboration with an artist, I translated photographs of their actual studio into a miniature rendition. Throughout the making process, I distorted and rearranged existing elements, causing the space to resemble something cut and reassembled through the lens of memory.
