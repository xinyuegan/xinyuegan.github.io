Filling the Riverbed

Installation / 2026

Statement

I braided palm leaves into a long rope, stretching from the ends of my hair all the way down to my feet.

This braid settled perfectly into the hollow of my spine, the crease of my buttocks, the grooves of my groin, the gaps between my legs, and the space between my feet—as if a riverbed had been filled with soil, raised to the level of its banks.
