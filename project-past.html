How the Past Hurts a Person

Performance / 2023

Statement

I place a ball of rice paper soaked with ink into a bathtub filled with sheets of rice paper. Using a showerhead, I pour water over the inked paper ball until the ink slowly spreads and saturates every sheet.

Then I paste these sheets onto a wall and project onto them the moving image of rice paper being soaked by ink.
