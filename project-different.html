We are different, we are all the same!

Documentary / 2023 / 6 min 50 sec / Color / Sound

Statement

"We Are Different, We Are the Same" is a documentary focusing on neurodiverse children, using green and orange to symbolize the relationship between children with special needs and their communities.

Through warm, authentic stop-motion animation interwoven with real-life footage, the film highlights the unique value of each child’s individuality. It centers on the journeys of Yifang and Boyu within an inclusive classroom, while also capturing the dedicated care provided by their families and organization leaders. In its closing moments, the documentary amplifies a collective call from across society for greater understanding and support for children with special needs.

Within every relationship, we encounter both difference and unity. To recognize difference is to appreciate the distinct qualities and richness of each individual; to embrace unity is to foster connection and empathy on a foundation of acceptance—building bridges between one another, in a journey that transforms both the helped and the helper.
