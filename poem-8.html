Meaning Spits the Owl's Eye Out

In the once and after, before all arrives,
I watch the blue shade beneath the orange skies,
Imagining some former rise and fall.
Bitter to the bitter, without taste,
Bitter to the bitter, without source.

Space-time descends in slow recurrence's tide,
My sense of progress lost, I hover near.
What forms the rim of sunset, spreading wide?
Are wings of withered birds,
like those that fly.
Owl-messenger,
In eye holds lever of the real and feigned,
Hovering, hovering.

Red lava sets, congeals, no longer flows,
Purple flame snaps its jaws,
with crackling sound.
Peace, in the struggle,
leaves a broken tail.

I watch the sunset scorch the withered plain,
A grand life's funeral pyre, all sinks to rest.
Absurdity spits bubbles in the molten vein,
Again, and yet again,
Decline.

What is it?
The mask we cling to, even at the end,
The cowardice inverted, yet won't fall,
Where being and definition bind and blend

It's meaning, spitting out
The owl's eyes.
