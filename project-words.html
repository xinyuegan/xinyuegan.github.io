The Day the Words Escaped

Experimental Film / 2024 / 10 min 13 sec / Black and white / Sound

Statement

I imagine that one day words escape from the page, turning into a mass of black shadows slipping into cracks and crevices.

Sometimes they become electrical wires cutting across the sky, railway tracks slicing through the earth, or raindrops shattering the surface of a lake.

We watch all of this happen.

We open our mouths, wanting to say something—

and suddenly realize that we cannot say anything at all.
